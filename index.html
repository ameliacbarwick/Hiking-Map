Explore the Scenic Hiking Trails of Banff National Park

About This Map

This interactive map showcases the breathtaking hiking trails of Banff National Park. It guides you through stunning landscapes, offering insights into the flora and fauna that inhabit this pristine wilderness.

The featured trail provides an exhilarating experience, complete with panoramic views, lush forests, and picturesque waterfalls. Each marker highlights significant points of interest, helping adventurers plan their journey effectively.

Designed for both novice and experienced hikers, this map serves as a valuable resource for planning your next outdoor adventure in one of Canada's most beautiful national parks.

Map Author: Amelia Barwick

For more information about hiking in Banff National Park, visit Parks Canada - Banff National Park.
